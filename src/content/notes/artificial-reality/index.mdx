My time at **Artificial Reality** was a period of immense growth and exposure to the cutting edge of digital experiences. As a creative content production studio specializing in everything from CGI and films to web applications and promotional materials, Artificial Reality provided a unique environment to explore how design and technology converge to create truly immersive content.

Working here meant being at the forefront of digital innovation, constantly pushing boundaries in areas like real-time 3D, augmented reality, and AI-powered interactions.

---

I had the privilege of contributing to a diverse portfolio of projects that challenged my skills in UI/UX design and frontend development. Each one offered unique learning opportunities and a chance to make a tangible impact.
