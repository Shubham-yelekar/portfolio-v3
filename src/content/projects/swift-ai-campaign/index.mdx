# Summary

For the launch of the highly anticipated new **Maruti Swift**, the challenge was to create a marketing campaign that would cut through the noise and build a strong, personal connection with potential customers.

The answer was an innovative, **AI-powered** experience called **"Become a Swifter"**.

---

# My Role

My primary responsibilities for the "Become a Swifter" campaign revolved around crafting the user-facing experience, ensuring it was intuitive, engaging, and technically robust.

- Designed the entire **multi-step**, **mobile-first user journey.**
- Developed the **web app in React**, including _camera API_ integration, **form/AI backend submissions, and ensuring responsiveness.**
- Ensured seamless integration of all **design, branding, and interactive components.**

---

# User Journey

We designed a simple, mobile-first web experience to guide users through the process. The journey was designed to be quick, fun, and highly engaging.

**Take a Selfie :** The experience begins with the user taking a quick selfie directly through their browser.

**Personalize Your Swift :** Users then provide some basic information, such as gender and their preferred color for the new Swift.

**Submit & Confirm :** With a final click to confirm, the user's data is sent to our AI image generation engine.
